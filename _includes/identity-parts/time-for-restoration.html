IT'S TIME TO BE FULLY RESTORED

If people have offended you and harmed you, forgive them from your heart, just as God has forgiven you. Matthew 18:21-35 explains the importance of forgiveness. Won't you read this Scripture now? God's forgiveness toward us helps us to do the same to others. Do not wait any longer but draw near to God in childlike faith because He is faithful to all His promises (Hebrews 10:19-23).

Strive for peace with everyone, and for the holiness without which no one will see the Lord. See to it that no one fails to obtain the grace of God; that no “root of bitterness” springs up and causes trouble, and by it many become defiled (Hebrews 12:14-15).

...as the truth is in Jesus, to put off your old self, which belongs to your former manner of life and is corrupt through deceitful desires, and to be renewed in the spirit of your minds, and to put on the new self, created after the likeness of God in true righteousness and holiness. (Ephesians 4:21-24)

Can you picture your Heavenly Father, stretching out His hands towards you and inviting you to receive forgiveness and healing? He wants to restore His image in you. Do not let hurts of the past hold you back. Come to Jesus today and allow Him to take possession of your heart. Through the gracious work of Jesus Christ, you can become a new creation in Jesus Christ! There is no other way to be renewed and restored.

Understanding who you are is the starting point for spiritual growth. May Jesus Christ, our Lord and Saviour, help you to find this treasure!

The Word of God usually requires you and me to respond. Let us not delay but be reconciled to everyone.

and forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. (Matthew 6:12)
