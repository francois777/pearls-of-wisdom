Christian Resources

Over time, I have discovered websites that are truly inspirational and informative. Some contain real-life stories; some have great educational value and some are ongoing Christian ministries that touch the lives of many people. I hope that you will find similar inspiration from these valuable resources.

The Christian Heart
About everyday people who live out their faith.

Read about true stories of ordinary people across the world who share their faith and testimonies. Some stories tell about the courage of people, overcoming unthinkable challenges.
