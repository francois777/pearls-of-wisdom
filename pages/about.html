About this website

Pearls of Wisdom for Today is built by the Christian community for the Christian community. It's purpose is to educate and inspire.

The author is inviting believers to contribute in various ways:

  • Inspirational articles
  • Bible stories for young children and teenagers
  • Testimonies of the goodness of God
  • Brief teachings suitable for most ages

The author also welcomes ideas to make this website modern, attractive and interactive to provide an enjoyable experience for everyone.
