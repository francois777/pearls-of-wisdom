Fundamental Doctrine of the Bible

Spiritual growth starts when the Holy Spirit enters a believer’s heart. The Holy Spirit, who is your Comforter and Helper, lives in you. He teaches you the ways of God and reminds you of everything that Jesus taught. He imprints in your heart, the character of God – Merciful, Gracious, Compassionate, Slow to anger, Abounding in steadfast love, forgiving iniquity, transgression, and sin.
